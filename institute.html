What This Is

A micro-think tank inside a story universe.

The Emergence Collective Institute is the "hard policy brain" of the broader Emergence Collective project.

  • On one side: novels, scrolls, and prophetic fiction
  • On this side: concrete constitutional proposals, governance frameworks, and system designs that could actually be implemented

We treat the books as mythic R&D, and the Institute as the place where those ideas get hammered into real-world architecture.

Why an Institute, Not a Movement

We are not:

  • A political party
  • A campaign
  • A personality cult

We are:

  • A small, independent, unapologetically experimental design lab for the future of democracy
  • Focused on AI governance, institutional reform, and economic/climate frameworks that don't rely on fantasy

Our work is meant to be stolen, adapted, criticized, and improved — by journalists, scholars, civil servants, and organizers who still care about reality.

Current Projects

Article IV — The Artificial Intelligence Branch

A constitutional referee in a cage made of citizens, courts, and unbreakable rules.

Article IV is our first full, end-to-end artifact: a drafted constitutional amendment creating an AI Branch of government that:

  • Has no power to rule
  • Only the power to analyze, model, and expose bullshit
  • Locked inside a cage of transparency, human supremacy, and citizen oversight

It comes with:

  • Full legal text (Sections 1-9)
  • Plain-English explainer
  • Stress test scenarios (climate wars, privacy breaches, political pressure, foreign attacks)
  • Methodology documentation
